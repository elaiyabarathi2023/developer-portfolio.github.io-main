Waste Recycler booking Website

JunkOut is a website which makes buying and selling of waste for the consumer easy and hassle free. It connects customers with the recycler. The seller has to be a registered customer on the site to book a vendor. To book the vendor the customer will have to add their address and approximate weight of waste to sell. Vendors will have a separate login which will notify them of the bookings made for pick-up and they can contact customers. The vendors follow the rate card provided on the website.

Automatic Temperature Check and Limiting Customer Entrance using IoT

The objective of the project is to automate the system of allowing limited people into shops or other places at any given time by keeping count of people entering any premises using Ultrasonic Distance sensor and contactless temperature detection of the person before entering using contactless IR Temperature sensor to ensure overcrowding in shops and ensuring everyone's safety.
